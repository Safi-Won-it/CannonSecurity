Who We Are

Founded by Raven Ramnarine, Cannon Security and Patrol Services operates on a core management principle: fostering close, flexible, and responsive working relationships with every client we serve. We look carefully at your unique needs to provide security solutions that are both highly effective and cost-efficient.

As a proudly ISO 9001:2015 registered company, our dedication to integrity, service, and professionalism is backed by rigorous international standards. In 2021 alone, our dedicated team of 86 field and management staff successfully conducted over 50,000 hours of permanent security coverage across the province.

Advanced Access Control

Elite Training Standards

A security firm is only as strong as its personnel. At Cannon Security, all applicants must pass a demanding selection process, including achieving a minimum of 80% on written exams and a favorable review on operational deportment before joining our team.
